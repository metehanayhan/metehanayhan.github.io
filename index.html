About Me

Hello, I'm Metehan Ayhan, a third-year Computer Engineering student at Necmettin Erbakan University.

I took my first steps into software development by learning the C programming language. Later on, I explored Python and Java, building simple projects along the way. After completing my core engineering courses, I decided to focus on artificial intelligence. Initially, I shifted from frontend training to data science and AI at the same institution.

During the summer of my second year, I attended online bootcamps that gave me a comprehensive introduction to AI, where I developed various projects and participated in Kaggle competitions.

Now, as a third-year student, I've been selected for the AI track in the Engineer Hub program organized by ASELSAN Konya, where we're currently working on image processing projects. I'm also researching large language models and Transformers architectures.

I'm passionate about learning and continuous growth. Feel free to check out my projects on GitHub and reach out if you have any suggestions or would like to collaborate.

Traffic Sign Recognition

This project aims to build a deep learning model that can recognize and classify German traffic signs. The model is trained on the German Traffic Sign Dataset, which contains various traffic sign images. The project allows users to upload an image of a traffic sign and predicts the class of the sign. The model is deployed as a Streamlit web application and has been published on Hugging Face Spaces.
